About Cork Parties - Best in Cork for DJ Hire, Bouncy Castles, Photo Booths & More

Welcome to Cork Parties, your one-stop shop for unforgettable event entertainment in Cork and beyond! Since our humble beginnings in 2010, we have been dedicated to bringing joy, energy, and excitement to every occasion. What started as a small operation providing DJ services for pubs, clubs, and private parties has blossomed into a thriving business offering a wide range of entertainment options for weddings, corporate events, and family celebrations.

Our Journey

We bootstrapped Cork Parties from day one, reinvesting every euro earned back into the business to expand and enhance our offerings. Our early success in the party scene motivated us to explore new opportunities, and it wasn’t long before we ventured into the wedding industry, quickly becoming a trusted name for memorable celebrations.

In our quest to bring even more fun to our clients, we made a significant leap by adding bouncy castles to our services. We purchased our first set of castles from BeeTee in the UK, renowned for their colorful designs and high-quality manufacturing. These exceptional products not only set us apart from competitors but also solidified our reputation for delivering top-tier entertainment.

What We Offer

Over the years, Cork Parties has grown into a comprehensive event entertainment provider. Here’s a glimpse of what we bring to the table:

  • Professional DJ Services: Whether it’s a lively kids’ party, an elegant wedding, or a buzzing corporate event, our DJs know how to set the perfect mood.
  • Photo Booths and Selfie Mirrors: These are some of our favorite services to offer! Capturing smiles and creating lasting memories, our photobooths and selfie mirrors add an interactive and fun element to any event.
  • Bouncy Castles: Ideal for family events, our vibrant and durable bouncy castles are a hit with kids and adults alike.
  • Corporate Events: For larger gatherings, we rely on a trusted network of skilled staff and talented entertainers to ensure your event runs smoothly and is packed with entertainment.

Why Choose Us?

At Cork Parties, we pride ourselves on providing a personal touch to every event. Our journey from a small startup to a well-established business has been fueled by passion, hard work, and an unwavering commitment to quality. We love what we do, and it shows in the smiles of our customers and the success of their events.

So, whether you’re planning a wedding, a corporate function, or a family celebration, trust Cork Parties to make it extraordinary. Get in touch with us today to learn how we can bring your vision to life!
