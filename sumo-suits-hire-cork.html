What are Sumo Suits?

Sumo suits are padded, inflatable outfits that make the wearer resemble a sumo wrestler. The objective is simple—knock your opponent out of the ring or onto the mat while trying to stay on your feet. It’s a fun, physical activity that’s sure to get everyone involved, whether they’re participating or just watching the action.

Why Hire Sumo Suits in Cork City?

  • Fun for All Ages: Whether it’s a kids’ birthday party or an adult gathering, sumo suits provide endless entertainment for everyone.
  • Safe and Supervised: The suits are designed for safety, and events often include padded mats and supervision to ensure everyone has a great time without any risks.
  • Great for Group Events: Sumo wrestling is a perfect icebreaker and a fantastic way to engage large groups, making it ideal for corporate events, team-building activities, or family gatherings.
